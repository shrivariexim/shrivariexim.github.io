What we SELL

RICE, the very essential food world wide, almost 90 % in Asian continent, and 100 % in southern INDIA. The consumption of rice increases day by day according to the population growth. Here are some Tips which we would like to share with you. The RICE which we consume are of different variety and quality. There are different methods of process according to people's need.Here are the details.

Methods of Process: RAW Rice, BOILED Rice, DOUBBLE Boiled Rice, STEAM Rice Etc.

These methods depend according to the needs of the people, quality and variety of the Paddy Grains. The grain of the Paddy has different Layers. With the help of today's modern machinery the layers of the Paddy is opened,removed and processed as the quality Rice.

All the above methods of process are carried at different parts of india,states and in the local area. All the process are Healthy and Nutricious for our Body. So. this is how we make our sales and keep the whole society Healthy.

Raw Rice

Here, The Paddy is dried in the open yard or dried with the help of a drier, cleaned with a paddy separator and the Hulled with a Huller were the layer of the paddy is removed. After removing the layer the Hulled rice is filtered by a De-Stoner, and polished by a silky polisher and sortexed by a sortex machine and packed hygienically according to the needs in PP Bags, Jute Bags Etc. This is a very simple process.

Boiled Rice

Here, The Paddy is socked in the soft water for a period of time. After the socking the paddy is sent for the boiler for steam, After this process the paddy is sent to the Drier and dried. The dried paddy is sent to the Huller and hulled removing the layers. The Rice which we get from the huller is sent for polishing,Destoned and sortexed. The sortexed rice is finally packed in different catagories and in different weights and Brands which is sent to the market for SALE.

Steam Rice

This is the latest and modern method. This is very Time saving,cost saving.By this method the raw paddy is cleaned and the steam is sent directly to the paddy seeds in certain temparature and dried. The dried seeed is sent to the Huller and the outer layers of the seed is removed and polished with a silky machine and destoned.Finally the rice is sortexed by sortex machine and packed in different weights hygienically and sent to the market for the SALE in different Brands. All these process are completely done by the latest machines.
